Fluff Stuff is founded by a group of knitting lovers in the local Pittsburgh Community. We specialize in throw pillows that are designed to turn any room cozy. Each pillow cover is hand-knitted from thick and soft hand-dyed cotton yarn in our artisan workshop out of Pittsburgh, PA.
